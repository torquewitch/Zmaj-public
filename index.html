Seasons Across the Continents

A meditative coloring book celebrating the beauty of our world — one season, one scene at a time.

From cherry blossoms in Kyoto to autumn forests in Vermont, this book invites you to slow down, look closer, and color your way through landscapes across the globe.

It’s not just a coloring book. It’s a quiet rebellion against the rush.
